About

Meetings

Chess Club meets every week during A and B block. Come to learn, hang out, and play games with your friends! We offer chess lessons and lectures weekly.

Tournaments

TJ annually competes in the Virginia Scholastic Championships and the National High School Championships. If you're interested in coming to these competitions with us, please let us know! Its super fun to compete, travel, and hang out with the team. There are also local tournaments almost every week, so let us know if you're interested in attending any of them.
